What is cyberspace?

Cyberspace

The best way to define Cyberspace is the virtual and dynamic space created by the machine clones.According to the Cyberspace definition, it is a web consisting of consumer computers, electronics and communication networks by which the consumer is connected to the world.

Safe Cyber Space

Precautions to be taken in Cyber space

• Be cautious while opening attachments or visiting links.
• Confirm requests for personal data.
• Keep your passwords secure.
• Guard your belongings!

Peaceful Cyber Space

Peaceful cyber space

Tax evasion, cyberbullying, and online libel are additional threats. For instance, the first cyberattack occurred in Estonia, where the malicious use of bots caused the websites of the Parliament, the Ministry of Finance, and several banks to become inaccessible. This highlights the necessity of fostering a secure and peaceful cyberspace.
